Mechanical Design:
Pololu Romi chassis base
Differential drive (2 wheels + caster)

Drive System:
2× DC gearmotors with encoders
Direct wheel mounting
Independent left/right control

Sensor Placement:
QTR-HD-07A line sensor at front
Sensor height ~ a few mm above ground(around 5)
BNO055 IMU near center of chassis
Bump sensor mounted at front

Mounting:
Standoffs and screws for components
Zip Ties for adjustable sensor mounting (height tuning)
Secure enough mounting to reduce vibration

Stability:
Low center of gravity
Weight concentrated over drive wheels

Design Considerations:
Minimize vibration and mechanical play
Compact layout
Easy access to components
Simple and modular design
